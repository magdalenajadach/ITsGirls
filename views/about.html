My name is Magdalena Jadach and I am a Software Developer

The Girls In IT web application was written as a part of developer mentorship program for women 'Learn IT, girl!'. During the course of the program I was guided by my mentor, Emanuele Libralato (you can say 'hallo' to him here.

For last few years I used to be a journalist in Krakow, Poland. Some time ago I became in coding and software development and after few months of treating this as a hobby I decided to switch my career path to become a programmer. Its certainly worth to chase your dreames, because they became true and today I am working as a Front End Developer in London, UK.

This application is a fusion of my two passions: code and news. I decided to build it to show IT stories from a different perspective - women in tech. I also hoped to encourage other girls and women to chase their own dreams and perhaps, get them interested in IT.

IT's a great journey!
